Croatian National Cuisine

Experience the flavors of Croatia!

Croatian cuisine is a delightful fusion of Mediterranean, Central European, and Balkan influences.

Some iconic Croatian dishes include:

  • 🐟 Grilled fish and seafood from the Adriatic Sea
  • 🍲 Peka - a dish with meat or seafood, vegetables, and herbs cooked under a bell-like dome
  • 🥘 Pašticada - a traditional Dalmatian beef stew
  • 🍲 Brodet - a fish stew cooked with various spices
  • 🫔 Sarma - cabbage rolls filled with minced meat and rice
  • 🥖 Pag Cheese - a famous Croatian cheese from the island of Pag
  • 🍷 Quality Croatian wines like Dingač and Plavac Mali

Explore the rich culinary heritage of Croatia and savor its diverse flavors!
